## Critical operation ordering in GET /api/audio/chapter/{chapter_id}

Balance deduction is the LAST step, after:
1. ElevenLabs generates successfully
2. Mutagen measures real duration
3. R2 upload succeeds
4. audio_cache document inserted

If anything before step 9 fails, user is not charged. If the deduction itself fails after upload, user gets free audio — this is the acceptable tradeoff (brief-specified).

The 402 check happens AFTER generation (not before) because duration is unknown until the MP3 exists. This means ElevenLabs is called even if the user might not have enough balance for a long chapter. Acceptable at launch — mitigated by the fact that most chapters will be cached after first generation.

|---|---|
| `R2_ACCOUNT_ID`, `R2_ACCESS_KEY_ID`, `R2_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY`, `R2_BUCKET_NAME`, `R2_PUBLIC_URL` | Chapter audio upload and serving |
