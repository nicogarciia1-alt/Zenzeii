  kanji:      { strip, bg, badge, label: 'µ╝óÕŁŚ' }  // sky/blue

## Design decisions

**Top strip (`h-1.5`) over left border**: Chose a 6px horizontal top accent bar rather than a left border. Avoids Tailwind CSS specificity issues with `border-{color}` vs `border-l-{color}` ordering, and works cleanly with shadcn's `Card` component (uses `overflow-hidden` to respect border-radius). The top strip gives an immediate "full visual identity" feel consistent with Nico's brief.

**All vocab cards show category badge**: Previously only kanji cards showed a badge. Now all word cards show their category label (Verb, Noun, etc.) in the matching color, giving consistent scannable identity across the grid.

**`other` as default on category picker**: Per brief ŌĆö user can just tap Save without engaging the picker. "Other" is pre-selected.

**Flashcard center-content preserved**: CardContent changed from `flex flex-col items-center justify-center` to `flex flex-col` with a `flex-1` inner wrapper for the content. The centering is equivalent, but the wrapper allows the category label row to sit at the top without disrupting the center-alignment of the word/meanings.
